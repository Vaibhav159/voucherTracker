gavel The Verdict

The Infinia Metal Edition sits at the apex of HDFC Bank's credit card hierarchy. It is not merely a payment instrument but a gateway to a suite of privileges designed for the affluent spender. The card exudes exclusivity with its metallic finish and invite-only issuance.

Its strongest suit remains the reward rate, which effectively translates to a minimum of 3.3% on all spends, accelerating up to 33% on partner brands via SmartBuy. For the frequent traveler, the unlimited lounge access (Priority Pass for primary and add-on holders) coupled with low forex markup makes it an indispensable companion.
